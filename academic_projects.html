Critical Analysis of Pattern of Development for a Smart City Using Innovative Approach

A Co-curricular funded research project on traffic impact study for Chennai Metro rail project (phase 2) for the selected IT-expressway corridor has been carried out to forecast the future traffic conditions and pattern of movement of people through the corridor

Analysis of Pavement Condition Index for Sub-Arterial Roads

This research project emphasis on finding out a new statistical model to obtain the roughness profile of the pavement based on the available distress data, furthermore another statistical model has been created to obtain roughness values for various speeds during roughness data evaluation.
